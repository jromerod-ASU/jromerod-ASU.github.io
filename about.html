Who I Am

My name is Lucio, and I’m the owner and operator of LD Yard Maintenance & Clean Up, serving Tortosa and the surrounding areas. I’m dedicated to providing reliable, professional, and flexible yard care services that keep outdoor spaces looking their best.

What I Do

  • Yard maintenance and clean-up services
  • Weed pre-emergent
  • Small tree trimming and plant care
  • Flexible scheduling to meet client needs

My Approach

I believe in combining professionalism with creativity. Whether it’s refining communication for my business, crafting engaging ads, or exploring innovative campaigns, I aim to make my services approachable and memorable.

Values

  • Customer Satisfaction: I prioritize clear communication and reliable service.
  • Community Engagement: I enjoy connecting with my local community through my work.
  • Innovation: I explore creative marketing strategies to stand out while maintaining a professional image.

Beyond the Yard

  • Exploring horticulture and plant care
  • Engaging with pop culture, from Better Call Saul to Star Wars
  • Staying informed about policies and programs impacting my clients and community
  • Attending Catholic Mass
  • Raising my family

My Mission

To provide dependable yard care services while building lasting relationships with clients and contributing positively to the community.
